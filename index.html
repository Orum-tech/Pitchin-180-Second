The Core Philosophy

Why The Pitchin-180-Second ?

Why Founders Choose Pitchin-180-Seconds

Pitchin-180-Seconds is built to give startup founders direct access to active investors, funded incubators, mentors, and ecosystem leaders — all on one platform. The 180-second pitch format helps founders communicate their idea with clarity and confidence, without long decks or complex jargon.Founders from Tier-1 to Tier-3 cities get equal visibility, meaningful feedback, and strong networking opportunities — completely free of cost.

01

Clarity is King

We force you to strip away the jargon and focus intensely on the core value proposition. This is about respecting the investor's intelligence.

Why Smart Investors Join Pitchin-180-Seconds

Investors get access to curated startups that are trained to explain their business clearly in just three minutes. This format saves time and highlights the founder’s thinking, conviction, and execution ability — faster than traditional pitches. It also opens doors to high-potential startups from emerging cities, before they become widely visible in the ecosystem.

02

Why Companies Sponsor Pitchin-180-Seconds

Sponsoring Pitchin-180-Seconds places your brand at the center of India’s startup and innovation movement. Companies gain visibility among founders, investors, incubators, and decision-makers — both on-ground and digitally. Sponsors also get early access to partnerships, pilots, and emerging talent, while supporting meaningful entrepreneurship.

A Founder's Crucible

No lengthy backstories. What is the pain point? How do you solve it? How do you make money? This discipline transforms 'nice ideas' into fundable businesses.

03

Substance Over Style

Mastering the 180-second pitch means you can capture interest anywhere—from a coffee shop to a boardroom.
